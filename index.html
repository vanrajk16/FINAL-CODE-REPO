FAQs_

How many members can my team have?

The maximum team size allowed is 4 members.

Can I participate individually?

No. A team of minimum 2 members is required.

Is the hackathon online or offline?

PU HACK CODE 2024 is a 36 hours OFFLINE hackathon which will take place at college campus of Parul University, Vadodara

When will the hackathon take place?

PU HACK CODE 2024 will be conducted from 14th March to 15th March 2024

What is the last date to register for hackathon?

The last date to register for PU HACK CODE 2024 is 7th March 2024

When will shortlisted teams be announced?

The shortlisted teams will be announced on 9th March 2024

Will there be accomodation/food arrangements?

Oh-Yeah-Absolutely. You will be provided with accomodation and proper meals on both days. Thanks to our sponsors.

Are there any registration charges?

Yes to register for PU HACK CODE you need to pay Rs.500 fees.

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops and other personal items required for the competition.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student of B.tech/BCA/MCA from any University/College in India.
